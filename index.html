SMART TRAFFIC LIGHT DEMO

Smart Lights, Smarter Roads:
Revolutionizing Traffic with Computer Vision

Our project uses the DERT model to detect and count traffic vehicles, combined with the street width, to flexibly adjust traffic light timings in real-time. This method allows us to extend or reduce the duration of green lights based on actual traffic conditions, optimizing traffic flow and effectively reducing congestion.
